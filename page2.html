Traveling

Although I never took a plane, I am fond of traveling. I have a long list of places I would like to visit, I will list some of them in the following table:

Place Country Information
Alhambra Palace Spain It is a reflection of the culture of the last centuries of the Muslim rule of Al Andalus.
Taj Mahal India It was built by the Mughal emperor Shah Jahan to house the tomb of his favourite wife, Mumtaz Mahal. it also houses the tomb of Shah Jahan himself.
Colosseum Italy The largest theater built by the Romans. It was used to host gladiatorial shows as well as a variety of other events.
